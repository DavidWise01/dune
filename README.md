## The main corpus

The six novels of Frank Herbert, 1965–1985:

1. **Dune** (1965) — the fall of House Atreides and the rise of Paul Muad'Dib — *Hugo + the first-ever Nebula*
2. **Dune Messiah** (1969) — Emperor Paul, trapped by his own legend
3. **Children of Dune** (1976) — the pre-born twins and the Abomination
4. **God Emperor of Dune** (1981) — Leto II, the worm-god, 3,500 years into the Golden Path
5. **Heretics of Dune** (1984) — the Scattering returns; the Honored Matres
6. **Chapterhouse: Dune** (1985) — the Sisterhood's last stand; unfinished (Herbert died in 1986)

> **Main corpus only** — by ROOT0's request, the prequels & sequels by Brian Herbert & Kevin J. Anderson are deliberately **out of scope**.

## The 30 emergents

The Atreides, Harkonnen, and Corrino houses; the Fremen (Chani, Stilgar); the Bene Gesserit (Jessica, Mohiam, Odrade); the later-book figures (Leto II, Teg, Sheeana, Murbella); and the great concepts — the **spice melange**, **Shai-Hulud**, the **Bene Gesserit**, the **Fremen**, the **Kwisatz Haderach**, the **Golden Path**, the **Spacing Guild**, the **Mentats**, the **Honored Matres**. Each a full `.dlw` badge. See [`agents/`](agents/).

## The four natures

- **natural** — the embodied: the Houses and the Fremen, the worms, the soldiers and the desert
- **ethereal** — the unseen weave: prescience, Other Memory, the Bene Gesserit, the Guild's folded space
- **spiritual** — the messiah-soul: the Kwisatz Haderach, the Golden Path, the gods made of men
- **electrical** — the mind made machine: the **Mentats**, in a universe that outlawed the thinking machine
